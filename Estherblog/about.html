About Me

I'm Esther Ramonki, an online journalist dedicated to uncovering the truth
and telling stories that matter. With a background in investigative reporting,
I strive to make a positive impact through my work by shedding light on important
issues and advocating for social change.

Experience

Over the years as an ungoing graduate, I've had the privilege of working with reputable news organizations
and media outlets, where I've honed my skills as a journalist and storyteller. My
experience includes conducting in-depth research, interviewing key sources, and
producing compelling content across various platforms.

Mission

My mission is to empower and inform audiences through responsible and ethical journalism.
I believe in the power of storytelling to inspire change and create a more informed and
empathetic society. By amplifying diverse voices and shining a light on underreported
issues, I aim to contribute to a more just and equitable world.
